Julia O'Hara Stiles (born March 28, 1981) is an American actress known for her roles in various films and TV shows. Born and raised in New York City, Stiles began her acting career at the age of 11. She made her film debut in I Love You, I Love You Not (1996) and gained recognition for her lead role in Wicked (1998), winning the Karlovy Vary Film Festival Award for Best Actress. Stiles rose to prominence with her performances in teen films like 10 Things I Hate About You (1999), Down to You (2000), and Save the Last Dance (2001). She has received accolades including a Teen Choice Award and two MTV Movie Awards. Stiles also appeared in films such as The Business of Strangers (2001), Mona Lisa Smile (2003), The Omen (2006), and the Bourne franchise (2002–2016) as Nicky Parsons. Her other notable film credits include Hamlet and State and Main (both 2000).
