Another Selling Sunset star has announced they are now single.

Hours after it Chrishell Stause and Jason Oppenheim revealed that they had broken up after several months of dating, Jason‘s twin brother Brett Oppenheim announced that he and girlfriend Tina Louise had split up.

On Tuesday (December 21), Brett, 44, took to his Instagram Story to answer questions from fans about real estate.

One question he received was “Are you single?” to which he replied, “OK, this question has absolutely nothing to do with real estate, but I will answer it.”

“As of very recently, actually, yes,” Brett added.

Tina, a 40-year-old Australian model and owner of the Sugar Taco restaurant, also announced her split from Brett on her Instagram Story.

“Brett is my ex, but we remain very good friends,” Tina shared.

Brett and Tina first went public with their relationship in April, sharing photos from a romantic vacation in Mexico.

Brett and Tina mostly kept their relationship out of the spotlight, but she did make several cameos in season four of Selling Sunset, which was released on Netflix in November.
