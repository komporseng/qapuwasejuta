Catelynn Baltierra- Professional Career

At the age of 17, Catelynn gave birth to her first child with Tyler Baltierra. Carolyn Elizabeth, also known as Carly by her adoptive parents, was their firstborn. Carly was given up for adoption by both of them when they were teenagers. As a result, Catelynn and her boyfriend made their broadcast debut together in the same year, on MTV’s reality show 16 & Pregnant. The episode focused on the obstacles of becoming a teen mother, such as marriage, adoption, finances, and profession. She later starred in another reality show, Teen Mom, in 2010. She appeared in the series from 2010 through 2012, when it ended. She rose to prominence on this show alongside Maci McKinney, Amber Portwood, and Farrah Abraham.

They saw that getting well-made clothing for newborns and children was difficult, so they decided to open their own clothing business, Tierra Reign.
