What cards are in Obsidian Flames packs?

May 18, 2024
blog

The Pokemon Obsidian Flames set is a part of the Scarlet & Violet series, released on August 11, 2023.

This expansion set contains a total of 230+ cards, including 197 in the base set and 33 secret rare cards, along with a few promotional variations. The Obsidian Flames set symbol is denoted by (OBF) and the cards in this expansion set follow the format of XXX/197.

Base Set Cards

The Obsidian Flames pack includes a variety of common, uncommon, and rare Pokemon cards. Here are some of the base set cards included in the expansion:

  • Oddish (001)
  • Gloom (002)
  • Bellossom (003)
  • Scyther (004)
  • Shuckle (005)
  • Surskit (006)
  • Masquerain (007)
  • Combee (008)
  • Foongus (009)
  • Amoonguss (010)

These are just a few examples of the base set cards included in the Obsidian Flames expansion.

Rare and Valuable Cards

The Obsidian Flames set contains a number of rare and valuable cards, particularly the high numbered cards and the Pokemon ex cards. Some of the valuable cards to look out for include Charizard ex 125/197, Tyranitar ex 211/197, Charizard ex 215/197, Charizard ex 223/197, Pidgeot ex 225/197, Geeta 226/197, and Charizard ex 228/197. These cards are highly sought after by collectors and enthusiasts.

Secret Rare Cards

In addition to the base set, the Obsidian Flames expansion also includes 33 secret rare cards. These secret rare cards add an element of excitement and rarity to the set, making them highly desirable among collectors.

Type-Shifted Tera Pokémon ex

The Obsidian Flames set introduces type-shifted Tera Pokémon ex with a crystalline appearance, including a Darkness-type Charizard ex. This innovative addition to the expansion set brings a fresh and unique element to the gameplay and collection experience.

Trainer Cards

The expansion set also features more than 15 Trainer cards, each with its own special abilities and effects that can enhance the gameplay and strategy for Pokemon trainers.

Special Illustrations

Furthermore, the Obsidian Flames set includes dozens of Pokémon and Trainer cards with special illustrations. These special illustrations add a visually appealing aspect to the cards, making them even more captivating for collectors and players alike.

Booster Box Configuration

Each Scarlet & Violet—Obsidian Flames Booster Box contains 36 booster packs, with 10 random cards per pack, three of which are foil. This provides ample opportunity for collectors and players to acquire a diverse range of cards from the expansion set.

Release Description

The release description of the Obsidian Flames expansion set paints a vivid picture of the themes and elements present in the collection. It speaks of red-hot embers illuminating the pitch-black night, with Charizard ex surging forth with newfound powers of darkness.

The release description also highlights the Terastal phenomenon imbuing some Pokémon ex with different types than usual, showcasing the innovative and dynamic nature of the cards included in the set.

 

On the whole, the  Obsidian Flames expansion set offers a rich and diverse collection of cards, ranging from base set cards to secret rare cards, type-shifted Tera Pokémon ex, Trainer cards, and special illustrations.

The inclusion of rare and valuable cards, along with the unique themes and release description, makes the Obsidian Flames set a compelling addition to any Pokemon card collection.

The expansion set not only provides an exciting array of cards for players and collectors to explore but also adds depth and innovation to the Pokemon trading card game universe. With its blend of classic and new elements, the Obsidian Flames pack is a valuable and captivating addition to the Pokemon TCG series.
