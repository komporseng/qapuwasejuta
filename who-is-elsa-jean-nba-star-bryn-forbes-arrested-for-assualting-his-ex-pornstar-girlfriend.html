Who is Elsa Jean? NBA star Bryn Forbes arrested for assaulting his ex-porn star girlfriend

August 13, 2024

SAN ANTONIO, TEXAS: NBA free agent, Bryn Forbes was arrested on Wednesday, February 15 for assaulting his ex-porn star girlfriend, Elsa Jean. Forbes was arrested at a residence in north-central San Antonio. He was taken to the Bexar County Jail, where bail had not been initially set before it was adjusted to $2,500.

According to a San Antonio police report, Forbes had a verbal argument with his girlfriend, Jean, before striking her several times. Prior to the incident, the couple was reportedly at La Cantera Resort & Spa, and the argument started when they got home. The San Antonio Fire Department and Emergency Medical Services came to Jean's aid after a close friend reported the incident. Forbes has been charged with assault. No attorney has yet been named to speak on Forbes' behalf, and the case remains under investigation.

More about Bryn Forbes

Then, in 2021, he joined the Milwaukee Bucks as a free agent but returned to the Spurs in 2022, and he was traded to the Denver Nuggets midway through that season.

Forbes signed with the Minnesota Timberwolves in July 2022, but they waived him last week to clear a spot after acquiring Mike Conley Jr and Nickeil Alexander-Walker in a trade.
