The Office (2005–2011)

In 2005, Carell worked with NBC to star in The Office. Carell played the role of Michael Scott. However, the first season of The Office did not do well. Nonetheless, with the release of the second season, Carell got certified stardom.

Carell won a Golden Globe for his role in The Office. He received six Primetime Emmy Award nominations for his work in the series from 2006–2011.

On April 29, 2010, Carell left the show when his contract expired because he wanted to focus on his film career.
