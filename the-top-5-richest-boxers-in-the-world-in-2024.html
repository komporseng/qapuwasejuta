The top 5 richest boxers in the world in 2024

July 24, 2024
blog

Boxing has always been a sport that has captured the attention of millions around the world. The glamour, the glory, and the big paychecks have made it a sought-after profession for many aspiring athletes.

In 2024, the boxing world continues to be dominated by a few select individuals who have not only made a name for themselves in the ring but have also amassed incredible wealth.

Here, we delve into the lives and careers of the five richest boxers in the world in 2024, exploring their net worth, achievements, and the various avenues through which they have accumulated their fortunes.

1. Floyd Mayweather - The Undisputed Champion of Wealth

Estimated Net Worth: $400 million 

Floyd Mayweather, a former professional boxer and promoter, tops the list of the richest boxers in the world. With a career spanning over two decades, Mayweather's defensive skills and quick reflexes have earned him an undefeated record of 50-0 and a net worth of a staggering $400 million.

Notably, his legendary fight against Manny Pacquiao in May 2015 earned him a monumental $180 million, making him the highest-paid athlete globally.

Apart from his in-ring achievements, Mayweather's shrewd business acumen and smart investments have further bolstered his wealth, solidifying his status as one of the richest heavyweight boxers of all time.

2. George Foreman - A Boxing Legend Turned Business Mogul

Estimated Net Worth: $300 million 

George Foreman, known as 'Big George,' is a retired American professional boxer and Olympic gold medalist. With 76 wins out of 81 total fights, including an incredible 68 wins by KO, Foreman's boxing career has been nothing short of impressive. Though, a significant portion of his $300 million net worth has been attributed to his successful business ventures, notably the George Foreman Grill, which has sold over 100 million units and brought him significant wealth. 

3. Saúl 'Canelo' Álvarez - A Boxing Phenomenon

Estimated Net Worth: $250 million 

Saúl Álvarez, popularly known as Canelo, is a Mexican professional boxer and four-division world champion. His net worth of $250 million is a testament to his prowess inside and outside the ring.

Apart from his boxing career, Álvarez is a savvy businessman, owning a chain of gas stations, convenience stores, and his clothing line, which has significantly contributed to his wealth. Notably, his five-year $365 million fight contract with De La Hoya's promotion company and DAZN further solidified his financial standing.

4. Manny Pacquiao - The Pride of the Philippines

Estimated Net Worth: $220 million 

Manny Pacquiao, a Filipino boxing legend, is not only renowned for his exceptional boxing skills but also for his versatility in various fields. Apart from Manny Pacquiao's estimated net worth of $220 million, Pacquiao has enjoyed a successful career as an actor, singer, and politician, having served as a senator of the Philippines.

His iconic fight against Floyd Mayweather in May 2015 earned him at least $125 million, further contributing to his immense wealth. Pacquiao's multifaceted career has not only solidified his financial standing but has also made him a global icon.

5. Oscar De La Hoya - The Golden Boy of Boxing

Estimated Net Worth: $200 million 

Oscar De La Hoya, a retired Mexican-American professional boxer, rounds off the list of the top 5 richest boxers in the world with an estimated net worth of $200 million. De La Hoya's boxing career, which saw him bag 10 world titles across six weight classes, has been pivotal in establishing his financial prowess.

After retiring from professional boxing, De La Hoya ventured into a highly successful career as a boxing promoter through his company Golden Boy Promotions.

 

The world of boxing has not only produced some of the most iconic athletes but has also created immense wealth for a select few.

The top 5 richest boxers in the world in 2024, namely Floyd Mayweather, George Foreman, Saúl Álvarez, Manny Pacquiao, and Oscar De La Hoya, stand as testaments to the financial rewards and opportunities that the sport offers.

Their multifaceted careers, strategic investments, and business acumen have not only solidified their positions as legends in the ring but also as formidable forces in the world of business and entertainment.
